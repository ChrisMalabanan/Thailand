Thailand — Land of Smiles

Thailand is a Southeast Asian country. It's known for tropical beaches, opulent royal palaces, ancient ruins and ornate temples displaying figures of Buddha. In Bangkok, the capital, an ultramodern cityscape rises next to quiet canalside communities and the iconic temples of Wat Arun, Wat Pho and the Emerald Buddha Temple (Wat Phra Kaew).

It's the "Land of Smiles" and "Land of the Free": Thailand is known for its friendly people and is the only Southeast Asian country that was never colonized by Europeans. Its name, "Prathet Thai," translates to "land of the free".

Popular Destinations

Bangkok

Vibrant city life, street markets, and the Grand Palace.

Chiang Mai

Cultural hub surrounded by mountains and temples.

Phuket

Thailand’s tropical paradise known for its beaches and nightlife.

Ayutthaya

Ancient capital filled with historical ruins and temples.
